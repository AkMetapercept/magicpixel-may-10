# Delete a Qualification Criteria

You can delete a particular Qualification Criteria.

To delete a Qualification Criteria in Ingest Labs, perform the following steps:

1.	Select the Trigger that you want to delete.

2.	From the **Actions** drop-down menu on the top right corner, select **Delete**.

![deleteaqualificationcriteria](/images/qualificationcriteriaimages/delete.png)

3.	Click **Yes** on the confirmatory message that appears.

![deleteaqualificationcriteria](/images/qualificationcriteriaimages/deleteconfirmatory.png)

**Step Result**: The Qualification Criteria is deleted successfully.

![deleteaqualificationcriteria](/images/qualificationcriteriaimages/triggerdependencies.png)

**Note:** You cannot delete a Qualification Criteria if there are any dependencies in the tag. Only after deleting the dependent categories, you can delete the Qualification Criteria successfully.
Even after deleting the dependencies, if you cannot delete the Qualification Criteria, then you must Publish the relevant categories first and then perform the delete operation.
